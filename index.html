About

I am an Assistant Professor of Artificial Intelligence at the Higher Institute for Applied Sciences and Technology (HIAST), Damascus University, and the Syrian Private University (SPU). I hold a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence with a focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP).

My teaching covers both foundational and advanced topics in Artificial Intelligence, including Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Generative AI, and Software Engineering Design.

My main research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Semantic Representation, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI for Requirements Engineering (AI4RE). I actively supervise Master’s and Ph.D. theses in these areas.

My current research directions include semantic representation for long and complex sentences, multimodality, parameter-efficient tuning of LLMs, machine unlearning methods, and LLM-based steganography. I am also engaged in developing deep learning approaches for behavioral attack detection within SIEM systems, as well as AI4RE applications such as automatic code generation, technical debt detection, and intelligent software development tools.
