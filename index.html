Dr. Riad Sonbol

Dr. Riad Sonbol is an Assistant Professor specializing in artificial intelligence. He holds a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence from the Higher Institute for Applied Sciences and Technology (HIAST), with a research focus on natural language processing (NLP) and Semantic Representation .

He teaches advanced AI courses at HIAST, Damascus University, and the Syrian Private University (SPU), contributing to both undergraduate and graduate programs. His teaching portfolio spans deep learning, machine learning, natural language processing, generative AI, and several software engineering and design courses.

Dr. Sonbol’s research focuses on deep learning and large language models (LLMs), with particular interest in semantic representation, parameter-efficient tuning, machine unlearning, and AI-driven cryptographic systems. He is also deeply engaged in AI for Requirements Engineering (AI4RE), where he develops intelligent tools for code generation, semantic analysis of software requirements, and technical debt detection.

His academic contributions include peer-reviewed publications, systematic reviews, and applied research in Arabic NLP. He actively supervises graduate students and leads collaborative projects applying AI to real-world software engineering challenges.
