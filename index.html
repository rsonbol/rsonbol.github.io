About

I’m an Assistant Professor of Artificial Intelligence at the Higher Institute for Applied Sciences and Technology (HIAST), Damascus University , and the Syrian Private University (SPU). I hold a Ph.D. in AI and Natural Language Processing.

My teaching spans foundational and advanced AI courses—deep learning, machine learning, natural language processing, generative AI, and software-engineering design. I’m passionate about guiding students through hands-on projects that bridge theory and real-world AI applications.

Main research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning (ML), Deep Learning (DL), Semantic Representation, Large Language Models (LLMs), and AI4RE (AI for Requirements Engineering).

My recent research works focus on semantic representation for long sentences, parameter-efficient tuning, machine unlearning, LLM-driven cryptographic systems, DL approaches to detect behavioural attacks in SIEM systems, in addition to various researches related to AI for Requirements Engineering (AI4RE)including code generation and technical debt detection.

Research Interests

  • Embedding and Semantic Representation
  • Requirements Engineering (NLP4RE)
  • Arabic NLP
